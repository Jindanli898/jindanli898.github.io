Research

Residual Learning for AIMC Training. I study a residual representation across multiple analog tiles to address limited conductance states and asymmetric update dynamics in AIMC. The key idea is to decompose weight updates into a set of residual components with geometric scaling so that each tile operates within a stable dynamic range, effectively increasing precision and robustness under device non-idealities. I implement end-to-end training with AIHWKit(-Lightning) and evaluate accuracy/efficiency under realistic noise models.

Code: github.com/kdhhewuciedu/AIMC
