Research

Research interests: Analog In-Memory Computing (AIMC), wireless communication.

Residual Learning for AIMC Training. My recent work focuses on a residual learning strategy across multiple analog tiles to overcome the challenges of limited conductance states and asymmetric update dynamics in AIMC. The key idea is to decompose weight updates into residual components with geometric scaling, ensuring each tile operates within a stable dynamic range. This approach improves effective precision and robustness under device non-idealities. Implementation and experiments are available in my code repository: github.com/kdhhewuciedu/AIMC.
